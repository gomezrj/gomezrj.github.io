Lecture notes

During my time as a student I liked to type lecture notes à la Gilles Castel. You can find some of them below.

Functional Analysis II

Lecture notes for a semester course given by Prof. Marc Burger at ETH during spring semester 2022. Contents include Banach algebras, spectral theory and Fourier analysis on locally compact abelian groups.

Harmonic Analysis

Lecture notes for a semester course by Prof. Joachim Krieger at EPFL during spring semester 2024. Topics include Fourier theory, aspects in number theory and the Calderón-Zygmund theory.

Algebraic Topology

Lecture notes for a semester course on algebraic topology by Prof. Will J. Merry at ETH during autumn semester 2021. Topics include the fundamental group, singular homology and cohomology.
