Algebraic Topology

Lecture notes for a one semester course on algebraic topology by professor Will J. Merry at ETH during autumn semester 2021. Topics include the fundamental group, singular homology and cohomology.

Functional Analysis II

Lecture notes for a one semester course on functional analysis by professor Marc Burger at ETH during spring semester 2022. Topics include Banach algebras, spectral theory and Fourier analysis on locally compact abelian groups.
