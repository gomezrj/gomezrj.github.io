QED

QED is a mathematics student association at Universidad Aut贸noma de Madrid (UAM). It aims to bring people and mathematics closer together through many different activities and events. These include tournaments, talks by mathematicians from UAM and other universities, as well as students from all around the world, and the QED magazine, where students are encouraged to write articles about what they find interesting in mathematics or related to mathematics. It also contains mathematical challenges, interesting problems and interviews with teachers from different universities, as well as fictional interviews with several mathematicians from the past.

This project was born during the summer of 2021, and I am proud to be a part of it from the beginning. As of right now, I have written some articles for the magazine, which will be available here and in the list below.
