Theses

Bachelor's

This is my bachelor's thesis. It shows results for Hausdorff dimension of self-similar sets, the relation between Hausdorff dimension and the Fourier transform and an application to a distance set problem in geometric measure theory. (ES)

Semester Papers

Stability of Isoperimetric Inequalities

This is a semester paper I wrote at ETH, supervised by Prof. Alessio Figalli. In it we study the stability of the classic and anisotropic isoperimetric inequalities in the Euclidean space, as appears in the papers by N. Fusco, F. Maggi and A. Pratelli, and A. Figalli, F. Maggi and A. Pratelli.

The Faber-Krahn inequality for the STFT

This is a semester paper I wrote at ETH, supervised by Prof. Alessio Figalli and Dr. João P. G. Ramos. In it we provide the details and preliminaries of a recent paper by F. Nicola and P. Tilli showing an analogue of the Faber-Krahn inequality in the context of time-frequency analysis.
