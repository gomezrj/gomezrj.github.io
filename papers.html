Theses

Bachelor's

This is my bachelor's thesis. It shows results for Hausdorff dimension of self-similar sets, the relation between Hausdorff dimension and the Fourier transform and an application to a distance set problem in geometric measure theory. (ES)

Semester Papers

Stability of Isoperimetric Inequalities

This is a semester paper I wrote at ETH, supervised by Professor Alessio Figalli. In it I study the stability of the classic and anisotropic isoperimetric inequalities in the Euclidean space, as appears in the papers by N. Fusco, F. Maggi and A. Pratelli, and A. Figalli, F. Maggi and A. Pratelli.

Science communication

Visualising quotients

This brief article by Luis Sánchez Polo and myself is our contribution to the fourth edition of the twelfth year of the mathematical carnival. It covers the topic of quotient sets in various contexts. (ES)
