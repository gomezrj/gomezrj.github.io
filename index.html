About me

I am a PhD student at EPFL under the supervision of Prof. Joachim Krieger. My research interests lie on harmonic analysis and partial differential equations. Specifically, I have worked on

  • Fourier uncertainty principles.
  • Stability estimates for functional and geometric inequalities.
  • Quantitative aspects in dispersive PDEs.
  • Time-frequency analysis and signal processing.

I obtained my master's degree in mathematics at ETH Zurich in 2023, and my bachelor's degree in mathematics at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid in 2021.
